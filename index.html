Eduardo Vernier

I (will soon) hold a joint PhD degree from the universities of Groningen and Rio Grande do Sul for my research on visualization methods for hierarchical and high-dimensional data. I focused on dynamic treemaps and dynamic projection techniques, creating comprehensive evaluations, developing state-of-the-art methods, and applying these in the context of hyperkinetic movement disorders.

My advisors are Prof. Alexandru Telea and Prof. João Comba.
